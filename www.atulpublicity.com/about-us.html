About Us

Advertisement & Media Agency

Our journey began in Bhopal in 1988, under the mentorship of Mr. Rajesh and Vivek Jain. By June 1991, Atul Jain took the reins in Raipur, and today, the legacy is skillfully carried forward by the accomplished Anant and Akshay Jain.

At Atul Publicity, we specialize in diverse projects across national and international print and digital media platforms.

With a rich portfolio that includes collaborations with hundreds of customers and associations with renowned brands such as Forbes, Dainik Bhaskar, Times of India, Navbharat, IBC24, Zee News, News18, and many more.

Over the past 33 years, we have actively left our mark in Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Telangana, establishing physical offices in Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and Rajasthan. Explore our journey and discover the impact of Atul Publicity in the dynamic landscape of media and communications.
